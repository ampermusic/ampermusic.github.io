Drew Silverstein

composer | entrepreneur

Drew Silverstein is a composer, arranger, producer, conductor, and songwriter for film, television, recording artists, video games, and performance. From Atlanta, Georgia, Drew studied with Emmy award-winning composer Eddie Horst. Drew continued his studies in Nashville, Tennessee at Vanderbilt University's Blair School of Music with Michael Rose, Michael Slayton, Michael Kurek, and Stan Link. He has worked with artists including Christopher Lennertz, Timothy Michael Wynn, Philip White, Jim Gray, and Kristin Wilkinson. Drew currently works in Los Angeles, CA at Sonic Fuel Studios. Apart from music, Drew enjoys lacrosse, traveling, chess, and reading.
